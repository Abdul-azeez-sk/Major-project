Smart Harvest Xpert

Empowering Farmers with Data-Driven Harvest Decisions

Introduction

Agriculture is one of the most crucial industries, yet farmers often face challenges in determining the best time to harvest their crops for maximum profit. This project integrates machine learning, IoT-based real-time sensor data, and predictive analytics to help farmers optimize their harvesting window while maximizing revenue by forecasting market price trends.

Project Overview

The project focuses on three key components:

  • Price Trend Prediction using Machine Learning
  • Strategic Harvesting Window Optimization
  • Real-Time Sensor Monitoring through IoT

These components work together to enhance decision-making, ensuring crops are harvested at the right time while maintaining quality and profitability.

1. Price Trend Prediction using Machine Learning

Market price fluctuations significantly impact farmers' income. To address this, we use models that consider:

  • Historical market prices
  • Weather conditions
  • Government policies
  • Supply and demand trends

How it Works:

  • Collect market data from agricultural sources and APIs
  • Preprocess and apply time-series models like LSTM, ARIMA, or Prophet
  • Provide forecast-based price insights for better harvesting decisions

Example: If wheat price is projected to rise by 10% in 2 weeks, harvesting can be delayed for better profit.

2. Harvest Window Optimization

Harvest time depends on:

  • Market price trends
  • Crop maturity
  • Weather forecasts
  • Storage capabilities

How it Works:

  • Optimization models suggest best harvest times
  • Alerts sent via dashboard or app
  • Logistics and supply chain factored in

Example: If stable temperature and rising prices are expected, the system recommends delaying harvest.
